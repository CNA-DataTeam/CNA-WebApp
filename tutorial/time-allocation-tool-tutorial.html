CNA Console · Quick Start

Time Allocation Tool — logging your time

Five steps. Your name and department fill in automatically — you just enter accounts, time, and channel.

  1. 1
    Open CNA Console
    In the CNA Console sidebar, click Time Allocation Tool.
  2. 2
    Pick a day
    Click the day on the calendar that you want to enter time for.
  3. 3
    Choose an account
    Pick an account name or customer code — the other fills in for you.
  4. 4
    Set time & channel
    Choose Hours and Minutes, then pick a Channel. One account per row — use Add Row for more.
  5. 5
    Save
    Click Save Allocation, check the preview, then Confirm.
Keep in mind
  • Unsaved entries will be lost — do not forget to save your time entries.
  • To fix a day, click it again, edit the rows, and save once more.
  • You can edit your entries for any day in the current period — previous period entries may only be edited within a short grace window after each period ends.
